Refurbished Inkjet Printer

Refurbished Videojet / willett / Domino / Linx / Leadtech / Hitachi / Alphajet / Continuous Inkjet Printer small-character continuous industrial inkjet printer sets a higher standard in marking and coding performance for maximum uptime. Designed for medium-duty applications that involve printing codes for multiple shifts per day and between 1 to 5 lines of prints. The ìndustrial inkjet printer is ideally suited for a range of industries including dairy, salty snacks, meat and poultry, cosmetics, building materials and much more.

Printer features

They are quieter in operation than impact dot matrix or daisywheel printers.
They can print finer, smoother details through higher resolution. Consumer inkjet printers with photographic-quality printing are widely available.
In comparison to technologies like thermal wax, dye sublimation, and laser printing, inkjets have the advantage of practically no warm-up time, and often lower cost.
Some types of industrial inkjet printers are now capable of printing at very high speeds, in wide formats, or for a variety of industrial applications ranging from signage, textiles, ceramics, and 3-D printing into biomedical applications and conductive circuitry.
